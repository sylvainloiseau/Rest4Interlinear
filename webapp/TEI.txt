  All other tiers goes into standOff element, after the <text>.
  The main segmentation present here may be any tier that belong to a type that is TIME_ALIGNABLE

   - The tier at the root of the tiers hierarchy (that may no contain the primary phonetic transcription).
  The tier is represented (as all the other tier in standOff below) as <span>s in a <spanGrp>.
  This tier may not contain any annotation. Then, the root element for annotation in tier cannot be u, or even ab.
